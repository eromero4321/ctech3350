Aunthetic Mexican Coffee Shop

Reviews

Dee Renee

Staff here are super friendly and helpful with making a great drink! She made the best hot tea with milk, cinnamon and another flavor. It paired perfectly with the pastry! The pastry was wonderful heated with a soft Fluffy texture and just the perfect amount of sweetness. The prices are much more affordable here, so try a few items! I love the vibe, calm and quiet but also kid friendly with outdoor and indoor spaces and toys to entertain the little ones. The patio was very large and a nice area to relax in. Highly recommend this location to escape the chain spots and support local! Kid-friendliness: Toys for toddlers

 

Mia Garcia

This place is so cute, such a homey vibe. It has so much space inside and they also have covered outdoor seating. Natalie was so nice! I got a Tres Leches Latte ($5.41) and of course it’s sweet but the coffee flavor is there! I also got a taco and it’s small but the price wasn’t so bad ($2.59) :) I definitely will visit again to try more menu items but also for the comfort the place gives! ✨ I went back to try the Matcha, I got vanilla and oat-milk with it. It was very good and creamy! And the price was good ($6.84)

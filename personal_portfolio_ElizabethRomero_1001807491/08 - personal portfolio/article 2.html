Law of Uniform Connectedness

When there are icons or texts that are inline to each other it shows that they are connected. One example could be the page number in which you are that is shown at the bottom of your google search results. It gives the user some clarity and uniformity. It also helps whenever it is a application for Fafsa or a job application it shows how many more tabs they have left to finish their applications.
